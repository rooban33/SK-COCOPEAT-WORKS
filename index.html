Benefits

Excellent Water Retention

CocoPeat has high water retention capacity, allowing it to hold water much better than traditional soil. This property ensures that plants have a steady supply of moisture, reducing the frequency of watering.

Improved Aeration

It has excellent air porosity, promoting better aeration for plant roots. The presence of air pockets in CocoPeat facilitates root respiration and prevents waterlogging, which is essential for healthy plant growth

pH Neutral

CocoPeat has a neutral pH level, making it suitable for a wide range of plants. Unlike some other soil amendments, it does not significantly alter the pH of the soil, providing a stable environment for plant roots

Sustainable and Renewable

CocoPeat is a byproduct of coconut husk processing, making it an environmentally friendly option. It is renewable and biodegradable, reducing environmental impact and waste.

Pest and Disease Resistance

CocoPeat naturally contains compounds that deter pests and inhibit the growth of harmful microorganisms. This helps to reduce the risk of plant diseases and the need for chemical pesticides

High nutrient-holding capacity

Cocopeat has the ability to retain nutrients and release them gradually to the plant roots. This property reduces the risk of nutrient leaching and ensures a steady supply of essential elements for healthy plant growth.
